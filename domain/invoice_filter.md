## 🔒 Security & Verification

### Domain Verification Checklist
✅ **Verified Payment Domains:**
- All domains listed are legitimate payment processors
- HTTPS encryption required for all payment communications
- Official domains verified through company websites
- Regular security audits and compliance certifications

⚠️ **Security Red Flags:**
```
paypa1.com              # Number '1' instead of 'l'
payp4l.com              # Number '4' instead of 'a'
stripe-inc.com          # Unofficial subdomain
paypal-verification.com  # Fake verification domain
```

### Phishing Protection Tips
1. **Verify sender domains** against official payment platform lists
2. **Check HTTPS certificates** in payment-related emails
3. **Be cautious of urgent payment requests** from unknown senders
4. **Cross-reference transactions** through official platform dashboards
5. **Report suspicious emails** to payment platform security teams

### Data Privacy Considerations
- **Sensitive Information**: Payment emails may contain account numbers, transaction IDs
- **Retention Policies**: Follow regional data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- **Secure Storage**: Ensure encrypted storage for financial communications
- **Access Controls**: Limit access to payment-related email folders

## ⚙️ Implementation Guide

### Priority Levels
1. **Critical (Permanent)**: License keys, subscription agreements, tax receipts
2. **Important (Annual)**: Purchase invoices, warranty documents, refund records  
3. **Standard (Monthly)**: Transaction confirmations, payment notifications
4. **Low (Weekly)**: Promotional offers, marketing communications

### Domain Customization
- **Active Platforms**: Only include payment services you actively use
- **Regional Additions**: Add local payment providers not in the global list
- **Business vs Personal**: Separate business payment domains from personal use
- **Legacy Services**: Remove domains for discontinued payment services

### Monitoring & Maintenance
- **Monthly Reviews**: Check for new payment service adoptions
- **Quarterly Audits**: Remove unused payment platform domains
- **Security Updates**: Monitor for domain changes due to mergers/acquisitions
- **Compliance Checks**: Ensure retention periods meet legal requirements

### Filter Optimization
```
# High-Value Transactions (> $100)
Extended retention, permanent storage for tax purposes

# Recurring Payments
Subscription management, auto-renewal tracking

# Micro-transactions (< $10)
Shorter retention, automated cleanup

# Refunds & Disputes
Special handling, extended retention for resolution
```
