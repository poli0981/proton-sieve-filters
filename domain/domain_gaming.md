## 🔧 Implementation Guide

### Priority Levels
1. **High Priority**: Distribution platforms, major publishers
2. **Medium Priority**: Gaming news, esports organizations  
3. **Low Priority**: Key resellers, mobile gaming

### Regional Considerations
- **Western Markets**: Focus on Steam, Epic, major publishers
- **Asian Markets**: Include mobile gaming companies
- **European Markets**: Add regional gaming sites

### Security Notes
- **Verified Domains**: All listed domains are legitimate
- **Suspicious Patterns**: Watch for typosquatting
- **Regular Updates**: Gaming industry evolves rapidly
