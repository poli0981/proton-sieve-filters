## 🔒 Security & Verification

### Domain Status Verification
- **Active**: All listed domains are currently active
- **Legacy**: Some domains may redirect to new platforms
- **Regional**: Availability varies by geographic location
- **Subscription**: Most platforms require paid subscriptions

### Common Domain Changes
⚠️ **Recent Changes:**
- HBO Max → Max (max.com)
- Google Play Music → YouTube Music
- Funimation → Crunchyroll (merger)

### Verification Tips
- Check official app stores for current domains
- Verify through official social media accounts
- Look for HTTPS certificates and security badges
- Cross-reference with platform announcements
