## 🔒 Shopping Security & Verification

### Domain Verification Checklist
✅ **Verified Shopping Domains:**
- Major retailers with established reputations
- SSL certificates and secure payment processing
- Official domains verified through company websites
- Customer service and return policies clearly stated

### Common Domain Spoofing Patterns
⚠️ **Suspicious Domain Examples:**
```
amazcn.com              # Missing letter 'o'
ebay-inc.com            # Added unnecessary suffix
wallmart.com            # Misspelled 'Walmart'
paypaI.com              # Capital 'I' instead of 'l'
target-store.com        # Unofficial subdomain
```

### Security Best Practices
1. **Verify Official Domains** through company websites
2. **Check SSL Certificates** before entering payment info
3. **Use Secure Payment Methods** (PayPal, credit cards)
4. **Read Return Policies** before purchasing
5. **Monitor Bank Statements** for unauthorized charges

### Red Flags for Shopping Scams
⚠️ **Warning Signs:**
- Prices significantly below market value
- Urgent purchase pressure or limited time claims
- Requests for wire transfers or gift card payments
- Poor website design or broken English
- No customer service contact information
- Lack of return or refund policies
