## 🔒 Health Data Security & Verification

### HIPAA Compliance Verification
✅ **Verified Health Platforms:**
- Major medical institutions (Mayo Clinic, WebMD)
- Established telehealth providers (Teladoc, Amwell)
- FDA-approved health devices and apps
- Certified genetic testing companies

### Security Best Practices
1. **Verify Medical Credentials** of healthcare providers
2. **Check Privacy Policies** for health data handling
3. **Use Secure Connections** (HTTPS) for health apps
4. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication** for health accounts
5. **Regular Security Audits** of connected health devices

### Red Flags for Health Scams
⚠️ **Suspicious Health Claims:**
```
- "Miracle cures" or "instant results"
- Unverified medical advice from unknown sources
- Requests for personal health information via email
- Pressure to buy supplements or treatments immediately
- Claims that contradict established medical science
```

## ⚙️ Implementation Guide

### Health Priority Levels
1. **Critical Medical**: Test results, prescriptions, medical appointments
2. **Important Monitoring**: Chronic condition management, medication reminders
3. **Fitness Goals**: Workout plans, nutrition tracking, progress reports
4. **Wellness General**: Mental health, sleep tracking, general fitness

### Customization by Health Needs
- **Chronic Conditions**: Add disease-specific organization domains
- **Fitness Goals**: Include preferred workout and nutrition platforms
- **Mental Health**: Add therapy and meditation app domains
- **Preventive Care**: Include health screening and testing services

### Data Retention Strategy
- **Medical Records**: 90+ days (important for healthcare)
- **Fitness Progress**: 21-30 days (trend analysis)
- **Daily Reminders**: 1 day (immediate action only)
- **Billing/Subscriptions**: 365 days (tax and insurance purposes)

### Integration Considerations
- **Health Insurance**: May cover certain app subscriptions
- **Doctor Communication**: Share relevant health app data
- **Emergency Contacts**: Include in health app profiles
- **Regular Reviews**: Audit health app permissions quarterly
