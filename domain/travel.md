## 🔒 Travel Security & Verification

### Domain Verification Checklist
✅ **Verified Travel Domains:**
- Official airline and hotel chain domains
- Established travel booking platforms
- Licensed travel agencies and tour operators
- Verified payment security and SSL certificates

### Common Travel Scam Patterns
⚠️ **Suspicious Domain Examples:**
```
bookng.com              # Missing letter 'i'
expedia-deals.com       # Unofficial subdomain
delta-airlines.com      # Hyphenated airline name
marriot.com             # Missing letter 't'
airbnbb.com             # Extra letter 'b'
```

### Travel Security Best Practices
1. **Verify Official Domains** through company websites
2. **Check Travel Advisories** from government sources
3. **Use Secure Payment Methods** for travel bookings
4. **Research Accommodation Reviews** from multiple sources
5. **Keep Digital Copies** of important travel documents

### Red Flags for Travel Scams
⚠️ **Warning Signs:**
- Prices significantly below market rates
- Urgent booking pressure or "limited time" claims
- Requests for full payment via wire transfer
- Poor website design or broken language
- Lack of customer service contact information
- No cancellation or refund policies
