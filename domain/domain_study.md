## ⚙️ Implementation Guide

### Priority Levels
1. **High Priority**: Your institution's domain, primary learning platforms
2. **Medium Priority**: Academic publishers, research organizations
3. **Low Priority**: General educational resources, supplementary tools

### Custom Domain Additions
- Add your specific university domain
- Include department-specific domains
- Add regional educational authorities
- Include professional body domains

### Security Considerations
- Verify educational domains before adding
- Watch for typosquatting on popular platforms
- Regularly update expired or changed domains
- Monitor for suspicious academic-sounding domains

### Retention Strategy
- **Critical (90+ days)**: Research publications, thesis resources
- **Important (30-60 days)**: Course materials, certifications
- **Moderate (14-21 days)**: Regular assignments, newsletters
- **Low (7 days)**: Daily study reminders, promotional content
