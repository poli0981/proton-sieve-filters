
### Phishing Red Flags
⚠️ **Warning Signs:**
- Urgent payment requests from unknown senders
- Misspelled retailer domain names
- Requests for personal information via email
- Too-good-to-be-true discount offers
- Generic greetings instead of personalized messages

### Safe Shopping Practices
1. **Verify Sender Domains** against official retailer websites
2. **Check HTTPS Security** for payment-related emails
3. **Cross-Reference Orders** through official retailer accounts
4. **Report Suspicious Emails** to retailer security teams
5. **Use Official Apps** instead of email links when possible
