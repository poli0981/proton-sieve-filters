### Phishing Red Flags
⚠️ **Warning Signs:**
- Urgent rebooking requests from unknown senders
- Requests for passport/ID information via email
- Too-good-to-be-true travel deals
- Misspelled travel company domain names
- Generic greetings instead of personalized booking details

### Safe Travel Practices
1. **Verify Booking Confirmations** through official company websites
2. **Check Travel Advisories** from government sources
3. **Use Secure Payment Methods** for travel bookings
4. **Keep Digital Copies** of important travel documents
5. **Monitor Account Activity** for unauthorized bookings
