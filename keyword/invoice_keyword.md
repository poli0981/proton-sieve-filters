## ⚙️ Retention Strategy Guide

### Critical Documents (Forever)
- **Software Licenses**: Essential for product activation
- **Subscription Confirmations**: Legal proof of service agreements
- **Annual Receipts**: Tax documentation requirements
- **Warranty Information**: Product support coverage

### Important Records (365 days)
- **Purchase Receipts**: Return policy coverage
- **Invoice Details**: Business expense documentation
- **Payment Confirmations**: Financial record keeping
- **Transaction Summaries**: Account reconciliation

### Temporary Records (7-28 days)
- **Transaction Confirmations**: Immediate verification needs
- **Refund Processing**: Dispute resolution period
- **Security Alerts**: Investigation timeframe
- **Fraud Warnings**: Response action period

### Marketing Content (1-3 days)
- **Promotional Offers**: Time-limited deals
- **Cashback Alerts**: Short-term incentives
- **Discount Codes**: Quick action required
- **Deal Notifications**: Flash sales

## 🔒 Security Considerations

### Phishing Protection
- Verify sender domains against official payment platform lists
- Check for HTTPS certificates in payment links
- Be cautious of urgent payment requests
- Validate transaction details through official channels

### Data Privacy
- Protect sensitive payment information in emails
- Regularly clean up expired promotional content
- Secure storage for license keys and activation codes
- Monitor for unauthorized payment notifications

### Compliance Requirements
- Retain business expense receipts per tax regulations
- Keep subscription agreements for legal purposes
- Store license documentation for software audits
- Maintain fraud alert records for security analysis
