## ⚙️ Customization Guide

### Platform Prioritization
- **Active Platforms**: Use shorter retention for frequently used platforms
- **Passive Platforms**: Longer retention for less active accounts
- **Business Accounts**: Keep professional content longer
- **Personal Accounts**: Shorter retention for casual notifications

### Notification Types
- **Critical Security**: 28+ days (account safety)
- **Time-Sensitive**: 1-3 days (immediate action required)
- **Reference**: 7-14 days (may need to refer back)
- **Disposable**: 1 day (no long-term value)

### Regional Adaptations
- **Western Platforms**: Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn
- **Asian Platforms**: WeChat, TikTok, LINE, KakaoTalk
- **Professional Platforms**: LinkedIn, Slack, Teams
- **Gaming Platforms**: Discord, Steam, Twitch

### Advanced Filtering
```
# High Priority (Security)
Account Security, Password Reset, Login Alert

# Medium Priority (Events/Invites)
Event Invitation, Meeting Request, RSVP

# Low Priority (General Notifications)
Like Notification, Follow Request, Comment

# Minimal Priority (Promotional)
Sponsored Content, Advertisement, Deal Alert
```

## 📊 Filter Effectiveness

### Expected Categories
- **Daily cleanup**: Social notifications, promotional content
- **Weekly review**: Events, platform updates
- **Monthly archive**: Security alerts, important updates
- **Permanent delete**: Spam and fake account warnings

### Retention Strategy
- **Security alerts**: 28 days (investigation period)
- **Events/Invites**: 7 days (decision timeframe)
- **Platform updates**: 5 days (adaptation period)
- **Social notifications**: 1 day (immediate relevance only)
