## ⚙️ Customization Guide

### Health Priority Levels
- **Critical (90+ days)**: Medical results, health assessments, lab reports
- **Important (28-365 days)**: Billing, security alerts, account management
- **Reference (7-21 days)**: Progress reports, educational content, analytics
- **Temporary (1-10 days)**: Daily reminders, promotions, app updates

### Fitness Focus Areas
- **Cardio Enthusiasts**: Running, cycling, heart rate keywords
- **Strength Training**: Weight lifting, muscle building, protein tracking
- **Wellness Focus**: Meditation, sleep, mental health keywords
- **Diet & Nutrition**: Meal planning, calorie counting, supplement tracking

### Health Condition Specific
- **Diabetes Management**: Blood glucose, insulin, carb counting
- **Heart Health**: Blood pressure, cholesterol, cardio exercise
- **Weight Management**: Calorie deficit, BMI, body composition
- **Mental Health**: Stress management, mood tracking, therapy

### Professional vs Personal
- **Medical Professional**: Clinical terminology, patient care keywords
- **Personal Fitness**: Consumer app language, motivational content
- **Corporate Wellness**: Employee health programs, company benefits
- **Chronic Condition**: Disease-specific management keywords
