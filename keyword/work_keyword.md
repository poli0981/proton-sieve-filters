## ⚙️ Work Filter Strategy Guide

### Retention Strategy by Category
```
📋 CRITICAL BUSINESS RECORDS (365 days)
- Financial documents: Invoices, receipts, tax documents
- Legal agreements: Contracts, NDAs, compliance records
- Important client communications

🔍 IMPORTANT REFERENCES (90-180 days)  
- HR records: Performance reviews, policy updates
- Business reports: Quarterly reviews, audit results
- Project documentation: Major milestones, deliverables

📅 OPERATIONAL CONTENT (7-30 days)
- IT notifications: System updates, security alerts
- Sales updates: Pipeline reports, customer feedback
- Regular project updates: Sprint reviews, status updates

⏰ TIME-SENSITIVE CONTENT (1-3 days)
- Meeting reminders: Today's meetings, urgent calls
- Daily notifications: Task reminders, quick check-ins
- Immediate actions: Deadline alerts, priority items
```

### Priority Response Guidelines
```
🚨 URGENT (Immediate response)
- Security alerts, system outages
- Critical client issues, legal notices
- Executive communications, board updates

🔥 HIGH PRIORITY (Within 4 hours)
- Meeting invitations, client requests
- Project deadlines, team communications
- HR notifications, performance reviews

📋 MEDIUM PRIORITY (Within 24 hours)
- Reports and analytics, routine updates
- Training notifications, career opportunities
- Regular project communications

📰 LOW PRIORITY (Review when convenient)
- Newsletters, company announcements
- Product updates, industry news
- General information, educational content
```
