## ⚙️ Customization Guide

### Content Prioritization
- **Time-sensitive (1 day)**: Live events, streaming alerts
- **Short-term (3-7 days)**: New releases, reviews, news
- **Medium-term (10-14 days)**: Books, podcasts, comics
- **Long-term (28 days)**: Billing, important subscriptions

### Platform Customization
- **Active Subscriptions**: Keep keywords for services you use
- **Inactive Services**: Remove keywords for unused platforms
- **Regional Content**: Add local entertainment keywords
- **Language Preferences**: Include native language terms

### Genre Preferences
- **Favorite Genres**: Prioritize keywords for preferred content types
- **Content Filters**: Add specific genre terms you follow
- **Creator Following**: Include specific artist/creator names
- **Event Types**: Customize for preferred live entertainment

### Advanced Filtering
```
# High Priority (New Releases)
New Episode, Season Premiere, Album Drop, Book Release

# Medium Priority (Recommendations)
Recommended for You, Personalized Picks, Top Picks

# Low Priority (General Updates)
Platform News, Feature Updates, General Announcements

# Minimal Priority (Promotional)
Special Offers, Limited Deals, Free Trials
```
